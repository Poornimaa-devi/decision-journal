# Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

## What is SDLC?

Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is a structured process used to plan, design, build, test, deploy, and maintain software applications. It helps developers organize the development process and produce high-quality software.

---

## Why is planning important?

Planning helps identify the project goals, user requirements, and overall structure before development begins. Good planning reduces confusion, prevents unnecessary rework, and saves time during implementation.
